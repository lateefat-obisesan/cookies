A web cookie (or HTTP cookie) is a small piece of data sent from a website and stored on your computer by your web browser. Cookies are designed to be a reliable mechanism for websites to remember stateful information such as items added to a shopping cart or recording your browsing activity helping to create a more personalized and seamless online experience.
